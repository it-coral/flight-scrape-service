Low Airfare Tips

Tips for finding the lowest mileage fare possible:

  1. It pays to plan ahead. The closer you are to your traveling day the more you pay. Why? Because airlines only allot a certain number of award seats for each flight (or based on supply and demand for revenue model airlines). Book early or those saver award fares might be sold out.
  2. Make sure to search PEX+ before you book.


Need a place to hunker down for the night? Note the following:

  1. Make sure you're hotel booking itinerary has your membership account for your benefits.
  2. Check the hotel for the terms of its best rate guarantee if it has one.
