About PEX+

We’re a tech company focused on making award travel and redemption better. We search other sites which allow award travel, including airlines, to show you all the information you need to make the best mileage and point travel decisions. With one simple search, you can view award airfare prices from numerous airlines in one place.

PEX+ is currently in "beta release" to demonstrate some of our capabilities, and we're constantly adding new features. Please feel free to use our site and give us suggestions on future improvements.

Hotel Owners

Do you own or manage a hotel or another accommodation type that you would like to appear in our search results? Please read below.

Show Your Hotel with Price and Availability

If you want to show award prices and availability in real time, and allow users to book online, your hotel will need to be connected to PEX+ via a technological interface. There are many companies on the market (often called “booking engines”) providing the services necessary to connect your hotel to websites and search engines like PEX+.

You can also contact any OTA we work with to be listed with them, and your hotel will show up in PEX+ search results.

Note: If you wish to delete pictures provided by one of our partners, or if you have any problems or suggestions along the way, kindly let us know.

Low Fare Tips

Tips for finding the lowest mileage fare possible:

  1. It pays to plan ahead. The closer you are to your traveling day the more you pay. Why? Because airlines only allot a certain number of award seats for each flight. Book early or those saver award fares might be sold out.
  2. Make sure to search PEX+ before you book.
